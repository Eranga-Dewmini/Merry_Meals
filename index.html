About MerryMeal

MerryMeal began in 2016 with a focus on “Healthy Happiness Near You” when a group of friends realized that local meal programs were struggling to put food on their tables for adults who need help.

MerryMeal improves access to healthy food through establishing innovative programming and partnerships to overcome barriers and strengthen the community food system. We believe that access to healthy food is a fundamental right. We envision a future where everyone in our community regardless of age, income, or ability has consistent access to fresh, healthy, delicious food. Through a combination of food rescue, kitchen and transportation services, we give food programs year-round access, while our direct service programs bring food to adults living at home who are unable to cook for themselves or maintain their nutritional status.
