About Me

Doyle has worked in the field of culinary arts throughout Maine, New Hampshire and Massachusetts for the past 9 years and has honed his skills at many fine-dining establishments during that time. He started out in the York, Maine, area, working at the Stage Neck Inn, the York Harbor Inn and Arrows Restaurant. He then joined a friend’s catering business, called A Moveable Feast, in Portsmouth, NH, and from there spent a decade as head baker at Café Brioche in Portsmouth. Doyle also spent two years as a baker at Beach Pea Baking Co., in Kittery, Maine.

Most recently, Doyle has shared his talents with Stonewall Kitchen as our lead prep cook and now as our full time in-house chef. In his classes, he hopes to pass along techniques, knowledge and insights that professionals can use to create incredible dishes.
